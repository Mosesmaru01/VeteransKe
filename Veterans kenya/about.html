About Us

Kenya Navy is a leading provider of maritime services in the East African region. We have been in operation for over 50 years and have built a reputation for excellence in everything we do.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and cost-effective maritime services to our customers while promoting sustainable development and protecting the environment.

Our History

Female Veterans was established in 1971 and has been a key player in the maritime industry ever since. Over the years, we have expanded our operations and invested in state-of-the-art technology to ensure we are always providing the best possible service to our customers.
